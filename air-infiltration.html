Air Infiltration Testing & Sealing

At Saving Energy Solutions, we specialize in comprehensive air infiltration testing and sealing services designed to improve your home's energy efficiency, comfort, and indoor air quality.

What Is Air Infiltration?

Air infiltration refers to the uncontrolled flow of outside air into your home through cracks, gaps, and leaks in the building envelope. This unwanted air exchange can significantly impact your home's performance:

  • Increases heating and cooling costs by up to 30%
  • Creates uncomfortable drafts and temperature inconsistencies
  • Allows moisture, allergens, and pollutants to enter your home
  • Reduces the effectiveness of your insulation

Our Air Infiltration Services

  • Blower Door Testing: We use specialized equipment to measure air leakage rates and identify problem areas
  • Thermal Imaging: We locate hidden air leaks using infrared technology
  • Professional Air Sealing: We seal leaks using the most appropriate methods for each location
  • Ventilation Assessment: We ensure your home maintains proper airflow while minimizing energy waste
  • Performance Verification: We confirm the effectiveness of our work with post-sealing tests

Air Sealing Solutions

Based on our testing, we implement targeted air sealing solutions:

  • Weatherstripping for doors and windows
  • Caulking for stationary joints, cracks, and gaps
  • Spray foam for larger openings and irregular spaces
  • Specialized sealing for attics, basements, and rim joists

Benefits of Air Infiltration Testing & Sealing

  • Energy savings of 10-20% on heating and cooling costs
  • Enhanced home comfort with fewer drafts
  • Improved indoor air quality and reduced allergens
  • Protection against moisture problems
  • Extended HVAC system lifespan

Our Simple Process

  1. Initial Assessment: We evaluate your home's specific needs
  2. Diagnostic Testing: We measure baseline air leakage
  3. Custom Sealing Plan: We develop a targeted approach
  4. Professional Implementation: We seal leaks using quality materials
  5. Verification Testing: We confirm results with post-sealing tests
